How To make Macaroni and Cheese

Description

One of the most recognizable dishes on the American dinner table Macaroni and cheese, when done correctly, has a smooth, creamy velvety texture with all the savory saltiness of cheese and the easily eaten pasta shells compliment it all to create a perfect balance.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Pasta shells
  • Grated Cheese
  • tablesalt
  • butter

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta shells by boiling them in salt water
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  3. Place pasta shells in pan layed flat and spread out
  4. Dump grated cheese in an even layer over the pasta shells
  5. Place baking pan in oven for an hour
  6. Take out and let sit until ready to serve
