How to bake Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

One of the most well known and familiar snacks in the Western world and all over the globe the easy to make but hard to perfect treat is one of the most loved snacks ever to be baked,the Chocolate Chip Cookie.

Ingredient Needed:

  • flour/li>
  • milk
  • eggs
  • butter
  • chocolate chips
  • tablesalt

Steps

  1. Mix the milk flour eggs and butter to create your cookie dough
  2. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F
  3. lay out cookies on baking tray
  4. place in oven for 45 mins
  5. take out and let it sit for 5 mins
  6. enjoy choclatey warm goodness
