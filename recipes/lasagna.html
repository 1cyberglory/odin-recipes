How to make Lasagna

Description

A classic staple in Italian cuisuine no Scicillian cookbook is complete without the Lasagna. Cheesey, saucy, sometimes meat-stuffed, baked to perfection you can truly make a whole meal in one dish.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Your preffered Italian herbs and spices
  • Ground beef or veal

Steps

  1. Lay the first layer of cheese down on the pan
  2. Follow the first layer with a layer of tomato sauce
  3. Follow the second layer with a layer of meat
  4. Pack all the layers in so it follows the shape of the oven pan
  5. Make sure your meat layer is seasoned well and tenderized
  6. Repeat these steps once more and close the top with another layer of cheese
  7. Preheat oven to 445 degrees F
  8. Once preheated place baking pan in oven and leave it for 2 hours
  9. After 2 hours take it out and wait for it to cool to a edible temperature
  10. Serve to family and friends
