How To make Macaroni and Cheese

Description

One of the most recognizable dishes on the American dinner table Macaroni and cheese, when done correctly, has a smooth, creamy velvety texture with all the savory saltiness of cheese and the easily eaten pasta shells compliment it all to create a perfect balance.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Pasta shells
  • Grated Cheese
  • tablesalt
  • butter
